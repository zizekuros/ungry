About Us

Ungry is a collaborative shopping list service designed to make grocery shopping simpler and more efficient for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that grocery shopping shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to eliminate the chaos of forgotten items, duplicate purchases, and miscommunication between family members and roommates. With real-time synchronization and simple sharing features, we help thousands of households shop smarter every day.

Why We Built Ungry

Ungry was born from a simple frustration: the endless "did you get...?" texts and the inevitable forgotten items on every shopping trip. We wanted to create something that just works—no complicated features, no learning curve, just a simple, fast, and reliable way to keep everyone on the same page.

Our Values

  • Simplicity First: We believe in keeping things simple. No bloated features, just what you need.
  • Privacy Matters: Your data belongs to you. We never sell your information or track you unnecessarily.
  • Reliability: When you need your list, it's there. Fast, accurate, and always in sync.
  • Accessibility: Everyone should be able to use our service, with a free plan that actually works.
