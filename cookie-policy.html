Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 31, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

Ungry uses essential cookies and browser local storage to provide the core functionality of our service. We believe in transparency, so here's exactly what we use:

Essential Cookies and Storage

These are necessary for the service to function and cannot be disabled:

Authentication Tokens

  • Purpose: To keep you logged in and maintain your session
  • Type: Session cookies and local storage
  • Provider: Supabase (our authentication service)
  • Duration: Until you log out or your session expires
  • Data stored: Encrypted authentication tokens, user ID

Cloudflare Turnstile (Security)

  • Purpose: Bot protection and security verification
  • Type: Security cookie
  • Provider: Cloudflare
  • Duration: Short-term (cleared when verification completes)
  • Data stored: Security challenge tokens

What We DON'T Use

To respect your privacy, we explicitly do not use:

  • ❌ Third-party advertising cookies
  • ❌ Marketing or tracking cookies
  • ❌ Analytics cookies from Google, Facebook, or similar services
  • ❌ Social media tracking pixels
  • ❌ Cross-site tracking cookies

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, please note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using Ungry, as they are necessary for authentication and core functionality.

How to Clear Cookies

To clear cookies and local storage from Ungry:

  • Sign out of your account (this clears authentication tokens)
  • Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" or "Clear cookies" feature
  • Select "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files"

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

Sets authentication cookies to manage your session. Essential for the service to work.

Cloudflare (Security & CDN)

May set security cookies for DDoS protection and bot prevention.

Your Consent

By using Ungry, you consent to the use of essential cookies and local storage as described in this policy. Since we only use strictly necessary cookies, we do not show a cookie consent banner.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to how we use cookies, we will update this policy and notify you through the service or via email.
